About Me

With 20 years of experience in global organizations and a decade focused on continuous improvement my work and myself, I am specialized in transforming Finance and HR operations through strategic alignment, data-driven decision-making, and operational excellence. I'm certified as a Master Black Belt, Agile Coach, and Scrum Master, and I continuously evolve my skillset to drive meaningful, lasting change.

I'm passionate about empowering teams and individuals to become strategic business partners by unlocking their potential through innovation, agility, and purpose-driven leadership. I can develop already built teams or build teams from the scratch in every domain of business based on my experience.

Specializations:

Continuous Improvement mindset: Designing and implementing systems, structures, ways of working, strategies, mindset for an individual, a team, a division or department that align with personal and business goals to deliver sustainable operational excellence.

Leadership: Leading and coaching into high performing, cross-functional teams to generate measurable value across every type of business landscape.

Strategy to Execution: Building a strategy for individuals, team, department and company and ensuring to bridge the gap between vision and implementation to have both ambitious and actionable plans.

Technology-Driven Transformation: Ensuring people and technology work together and support in identifying the type of automations needed

Stakeholder Engagement: Building strong, collaborative and relationships that foster transparency, trust, and long-term value creation.

What is my purpose?

To coach and develop individuals and entrepreneurs in their day to day business, to improve their activities, reduce and eliminate waste, and become better in everything they do with clear and tangible actions and results.
